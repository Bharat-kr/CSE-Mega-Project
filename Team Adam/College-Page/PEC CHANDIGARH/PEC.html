ABOUT

Punjab Engineering College (abbreviated PEC or PEC Chandigarh) is a public "research & engineering" institution under the administration of Union Territory of Chandigarh. It was founded in 1921 which focuses on the field of applied sciences, particularly engineering and technology.
PEC Chandigarh is known for its two-year and four-year programmes for which the entry is through the Joint Entrance Examination – Mainsand Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering.
It offers degrees such as Bachelor of Technology, Master of Technology, and a few others. It also has a comprehensive graduate program offering doctoral degrees in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics. NRI students and students from SAARC countries can also apply to PEC through DASA (scheme by Government of India).

Admission

  • One needs to take the JEE (Main) exam and pass the Class XII board examination.
  • Admissions are done on the basis of rank in JEE (Main) through JoSAA Counselling and CSAB Special Round.
  • Institute conducts it’s own counselling for Special Category Seats (Kashmiri Migrants, Children/ Grandchildren of Freedom Fighters, Sons/Daughters/Spouses of Military/Paramilitary Personnel and Sportspersons).
